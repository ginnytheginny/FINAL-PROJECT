Predators


Jellyfish are preyed upon by most ocean creatures. There are a lot of them so theyre an easy food source for whatever fish or turtle wants a bite.

Largest


The largest jellyfish (and one of the largest animals) is the lionsmane jellyfish. It can get to over one-hundred feet long and appears orange-yellow in color. They rarely kill humans.

Smallest


The Smallest jellyfish is the Irukandji Jellyfish, with a size of about one centimeter. Their tentacles can grow to much longer however, and they use them to sting tourists and fish alike. Irukandji is one of the deadliest jellies.
