If you find yourself suffering from a Jellyfish sting the best thing to do is clean it out(with seawater), remember to remove any stingers left over, or it'll just get worst.

Healing Help
  1. Clean with seawater
  2. Remove stingers
  3. Apply Vinegar or Rubbing Alcohol.

And for the love of everything good DONT pee on it.


However if the Jellyfish is a Man O War do NOT apply Vinegar because it could make the sting worse.
